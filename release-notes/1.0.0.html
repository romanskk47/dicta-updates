Dicta 1.0.0

First public release!

Features

  • Local Whisper transcription (large-v3-turbo model)
  • Hotkeys: Option+R to start, ESC or right-click to stop & transcribe
  • Recording timer overlay following cursor
  • 13 language support
  • Transcription history with copy/delete
  • Launch at login
  • Sound feedback (toggleable)

Privacy

  • 100% offline - audio never leaves your device
  • Zero telemetry
