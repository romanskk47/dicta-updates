Dicta 1.2.5

What's New

  • Subscription plans in-app — Activation now loads monthly, yearly, and lifetime offers directly from pricing config.
  • Plan-aware checkout — Checkout is now explicit per selected plan and currency, with stronger idempotency to prevent duplicate sessions on retries.
  • Billing portal resilience — Improved fallback behavior and clearer recovery actions when portal or pricing endpoints are temporarily unavailable.
  • Stability fixes — Addressed post-pivot regressions in grace-period handling, update config timing, and Sparkle integration edge cases.
