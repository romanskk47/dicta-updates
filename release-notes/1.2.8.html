Dicta 1.2.8

What's New

  • Improved Whisper long-form parity by carrying prompt context between chunks and locking auto-detected language after the first successful chunk.
  • Unified recording timer and audio buffer limits with a 60-minute cap for long dictations.
  • Fixed long dictation cut-offs by avoiding short-tail clipping and preserving text when word timestamps are missing.
  • Added explicit recording stop-reason diagnostics (`manual`, `escape`, `max_duration`, `error`) in production logs.
